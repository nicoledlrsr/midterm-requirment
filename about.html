Our Story

For over 100 years, Maybelline has been empowering women to express their individuality through makeup.

From Humble Beginnings

Maybelline was founded in 1915 by 19-year-old entrepreneur Thomas Lyle Williams, who noticed his sister Mabel applying a mixture of Vaseline and coal dust to her eyelashes to enhance them. He adapted it with a chemistry set and produced a product sold locally called Lash-Brow-Ine.

In 1917, the company produced Maybelline Cake Mascara, the first modern eye cosmetic for everyday use. The name "Maybelline" was a combination of "Mabel" and "Vaseline".

Today, Maybelline New York is the number one cosmetic brand in the world, available in over 120 countries.
