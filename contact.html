Frequently Asked Questions

Maybelline products are available at Watsons, SM Department Stores, Robinsons Department Stores, and other leading beauty retailers nationwide. You can also purchase online through Lazada, Shopee, and Zalora.

Always purchase from authorized retailers. Authentic Maybelline products have proper packaging with batch codes. Be wary of unusually low prices from unofficial sellers.

For purchases from our official stores, you may return unopened products within 7 days with original receipt. For department store purchases, please check with the store's return policy.
